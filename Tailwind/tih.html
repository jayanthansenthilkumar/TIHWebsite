About

Find Out More About Us

A hub for creativity and innovation, providing access to the latest technology and equipment to students. TIH will produce in-house projects for our college as well as consultancy projects. Project-Based Learning will be given to students, which will make them industry-ready.

Why TIH

To provide a space for students to explore their passion, collaborate, and develop industry-ready skills, bridging the gap between academia and industry.

To foster creativity and innovation through hands-on projects and real-world problem solving.

To build a supportive community that encourages learning, experimentation, and growth, while offering mentoring and networking opportunities with industry professionals.

Vision

To become a global leader in Technology Innovation by fostering creativity, collaboration, and technological advancements.

To inspire and empower the next generation of technology leaders and innovators.

To create a sustainable impact by driving forward-thinking solutions that address global challenges.

Mission

  • Promote innovation and entrepreneurship by providing a nurturing environment that encourages ideation, experimentation, and problem-solving.
  • Facilitate interdisciplinary research and encourage cross-functional collaboration across different engineering disciplines.
  • Continuously adapt and evolve in response to emerging technologies and industry trends, fostering the transfer of technology, knowledge, and resources.

TIH Outcomes


  •     Students will get project-based learning
  •     In-house and consultancy projects will be done from hub
  •     Trained students will get product-based company offer with higher CTC
  •     Access to industry-leading tools and technologies
  •     Networking opportunities with industry professionals

Selection Process

  • 1
    Primary Round
    Basic Coding Test
    2nd year students with CGPA above 8.0 can appear for this round. Questions will be based on basic problem-solving and programming.
  • 2
    Primary Round Result
    Final Round Eligibility
    Students who got above 80% will be eligible for the final round.
  • 3
    Final Round
    Personal Interview
    Eligible students will be interviewed based on their technical skills, communication skills, and area of interest.
  • 4
    Final Round Result
    Results
    A total of 30 students will be selected based on their performance in the personal interview.
  • 5
    Congratulations
    Welcome to TIH!
    You are now one of the Members of TIH!
